北京加达世纪

公司简介

加达世纪(Can-Reach Consultants)北京投资管理有限公司是一家专业从事海外投资、并购、移民、安家及置业的国际咨询公司，总部设在加拿大多伦多，同时在北京、美国、英国、温哥华、萨省、曼省设有公司。公司前身是由加拿大移民官及律师于1995年在多伦多成立的 Can-Reach Consultants 法务顾问公司，作为第一批步入中国的移民咨询机构，为世界各地申请人提供赴加拿大、美国、英国、澳大利亚等国的移民类和非移民类的法律咨询服务。多年来，成功为数以万计的家庭申办移民、完成投资及安家置业，形成了从评估、申请到安家就业一条龙的完善服务体系，同时还为客户解决在海外遇到的各类疑难问题。

公司由具有丰富办案经验的洋人大律师、资深的执照华人顾问（ICCRC会员身份）、上诉大律师、注册会计师及各专业人士组成的专家组，凭借二十年的丰富经验，根据各国移民及投资的法律、法规，为每一位申请人提供最佳的定制化方案。在与各国政府移民及经济发展部门的长期合作中，加达世纪以诚信、专业、周到的服务，取得了各个政府的高度信任，进一步保证了申请的成功率。

Can-Reach Consultants is an international consulting company that focuses on investment, entrepreneurship, immigration, studying abroad, relocation, and real estate purchases. We are headquartered in Toronto, with offices in Beijing, Vancouver, Saskatchewan, and Manitoba. The company is the successor to Can-Reach Counsel founded by Canadian immigration officers and attorneys in Toronto. As the first batch of immigration consulting authorities, we provide legal counsel service for those seeking immigrate or do business in Canada, USA, The United Kingdom, Australia, and other locations to worldwide applicants. We have successfully provided immigrant services for numerous families, as well as assisted with completing investments and relocation for years. This experience has allowed us to develop an ideal service for those seeking to relocate through our evaluation process. We also specializing in problem solving for customers abroad, finding creative solutions to challenges that arise for such clients.

Our company is constituted by an experienced foreign attorney, a senior licensed Chinese consultant (ICCRC Member), an appealing attorney, a certified accountant and several other professionals. With 20 years of rich experience, collectively, we provide the best solutions customized for every applicant in accordance with immigration and investment laws and policies of the country they intend to relocate to. In addition to a personalized plan that we implement for our clients, we have gained considerable reputation with the governments and their agencies that we deal with, thanks to long terms contribution to economic development, and a commitment to submitting only high quality applications. This reputation predisposes officials reviewing the applications of our clients to do so favorably knowing we provide sincere, specialized, and thoughtful service, which further ensures the success rate of application.
