北京帆之都

公司简介

北京帆之都教育信息咨询有限公司（以下简称公司）成立于1993年，是国家教育部、公安部、工商局首批获准专业承办赴国外留学和因私出入境服务的机构之一。公司通过20多年的发展，现已在全国20个省、市设立了分支教育机构，承办了数万名赴国外留学和移民的成功案例，签证成功率达96％以上。 Established in 1993, Beijing Fanzhidu Educational Information Consulting Co., Ltd (hereinafter referred to as the company) is one of the first batch of organizations approved by the Ministry of Education of PRC, the Ministry of Public Security and the Administrative Bureau for Industry and Commerce, to provide the professional service of overseas study and private exit or entry of the country. By means of the development of more than 20 years, the company has set up branch educational institutions in 20 provinces and cities in China today, and has successfully organized the cases of overseas study and emigration for thousands of people with a high success rate of visa application over 96%.

作为唯一与新西兰全部大学共同在中国招生的公司，我们与美国、英国、澳大利亚、加拿大、意大利、日本、法国、奥地利、德国、新加坡、爱尔兰、北欧四国、韩国、荷兰、马来西亚、阿根廷、巴西等国的500多所著名院校也建立了长期稳定的合作关系。公司在中国拥有200余名优秀的咨询顾问和行业专业人士，其中，90%的顾问都拥有海外留学和培训经历。他们从学生的角度出发，针对不同国家的教育状况和特点，为学生制定符合其实际情况的多种留学方案。

As the only company who jointly recruit students in China with all the universities in New Zealand, we have also set up long-term and stable relationships with more than 500 famous institutions in USA, UK, Australia, Canada, Italy, Japan, France, Austria, Germany, Singapore, Ireland, Scandinavian countries, Republic of Korea, Netherlands, Malaysia, Argentina and Brazil etc. The company has more than 200 excellent consultants and industrial specialists throughout China, and among which, 90% of them are experienced in overseas study and training. They can scheme out, on students' perspective, various kinds of customized overseas study programs for the students according to the educational conditions of different countries.

随着时代的发展，为了扩展商业服务的范围本公司在原有的因私出入境业务和留学业务之外，还申请了移民业务的资质。

As time goes by , our company has also applied Immigration qualification to extend business services.

移民业务范围：①为中国公民提供赴加拿大、新西兰、塞浦路斯、英国、葡萄牙、澳大利亚、希腊、西班牙定居、探亲、访友、继承财产和其他非公务活动；②提供信息介绍、法律咨询、沟通联系、境外安排、签证申办及相关服务。

The range of our immigration qualification : immagration , visiting relatives & friends , property inheritance service for Chinese citizens to Canda , New Zealand , Cyprus , Britain , Portugal , Australia , Greece or Spain. The sercices we Provided : information introduction , legal consultation , communication , overseas arrangement , visa application and related services .

2001年帆之都公司附属北京市西城区帆之都培训学校（以下简称帆之都学校）成立。帆之都学校倡导小班教学，80%以上的教师拥有硕士及以上学历，具有出国留学经历的教师超过85%。优秀的中外教师团队帮助学生全面提高外语能力和综合知识水平，尤其为准备留学的学生做好出国前充分的外语和心理准备。同时，帆之都学校积极引进美国、意大利、奥地利、新西兰、澳大利亚等国家院校的先进课程，为有志出国留学的学生提供省时、省钱的留学绿色通道。经过多年不懈努力，帆之都学校现已发展成为集少儿、初中、高中、大学和成人英语以及语文、数学、美术、音乐等课程培训于一身，业务涉及艺术生专业和文化课高考培训、国内各类语言考试及出国留学类考试辅导、冬（夏）令营、国际预科课程、企业培训和国际体育文化交流与赛事推广等众多项目的综合性国际培训学校。

The Fanzhidu Company affiliated Beijing Xicheng Fanzhidu Training School (hereinafter referred to as the Fanzhidu school) was founded in 2001. The Fanzhidu school advocates small class teaching and have a faculty that over 80% of them has master's degrees or higher while 85% of them have ever studied abroad. Our outstanding Chinese and foreign teachers are trying their best to improve the overall foreign language ability and comprehensive knowledge of the students and help those who will study abroad be well prepared in foreign language and mentality. Meanwhile, the Fanzhidu school has actively introduced world-leading curricula of the institutions in USA, Italy, Austria, New Zealand and Australia, providing time-saving and money-saving express approaches for the students who are aspiring for overseas education. Through many years' ongoing efforts, the Fanzhidu school has become a comprehensive international training school integrated with curricula and business projects such as English for children, junior and senior high school students, college students and adults, and additionally Chinese, mathematics, art and music, as well as the general course training of college entrance examination for art majors, the examination tutorship for domestic language tests and overseas study, the winter/summer camps, the international preparatory courses, the enterprise trainings and the international sports cultural exchange and competition events promotion.

二十多年的发展历程，为公司奠定了坚实的基础，我们将继续秉承"客户第一"的服务概念，以高度负责的态度、严谨的职业水准为有志赴国外留学和移民的人士提供优质、快捷、专业的服务。

Being laid a firm foundation by the development of more than 20 years, the company will continuously comply with the service concept of "customer first" and provide the people who want to study abroad or emigrate with our high quality, quick, easy and professional service with a strong sense of responsibility and strict professionalism.
